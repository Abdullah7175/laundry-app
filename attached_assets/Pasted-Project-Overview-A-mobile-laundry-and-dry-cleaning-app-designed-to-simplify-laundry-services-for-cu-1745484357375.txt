Project Overview
A mobile laundry and dry-cleaning app designed to simplify laundry services for customers across Saudi
Arabia. Initially serving your own laundry business, the app offers seamless booking, pickup, delivery,
and payment—all through a user-friendly interface. Once established, it will scale as a white-label or
SaaS solution for other laundry shops, helping them increase sales and customer retention through
digital transformation.
Target Users:
• Busy professionals
• Families
• Students
• Expats
• Small businesses (uniforms, linens)

User Journey:

Sign Up / Log In

Add Location (Auto Detect or Manual)

Select Services
o Wash & Fold
o Iron Only
o Dry Cleaning
o Special Garments (Abayas, suits, carpets)

Pick-up Time Slot

Delivery Time Slot

Payment (Cash, Card, Apple Pay, STC Pay)

Track Order Status

Rate the Service

Features (Phase 1 – For Your Own Business)
For Customers:
• Account registration/login (with OTP)
• Pickup & delivery scheduling
• Real-time order tracking
• Service selection and price calculator

Push notifications & SMS alerts
• Coupons and referral programs
• In-app support chat / FAQs
• Loyalty points system
For Admin (Your Side):
• Order management dashboard
• Fleet management (drivers and routes)
• Customer database & CRM
• Revenue reports and sales analytics
• Notifications and customer engagement tools
• Price management by category
• Service area management

Features (Phase 2 – For Other Laundry Shops)
• White-label or subscription model
• Multi-vendor management system
• Custom branding for each laundry
• Role-based access (Owner, Staff, Delivery)
• Commission tracking per vendor
• Integration with POS and local ERPs
• Mobile app for laundry partners
• Analytics dashboard for partners
• Review and rating management

Design Approach:
• Clean, minimal UI with pastel/blue tones
• Arabic + English support
• One-tap booking flow
• Map-based pickup tracking
• Visual icons for laundry categories
• Payment gateway integration
